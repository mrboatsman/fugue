Party mode: synchronized group playback.

One host controls playback; followers track the host’s state with sub-second accuracy via gossip heartbeats and clock offset estimation.

Sessions are ephemeral (in-memory only) — they don’t survive restarts, which is appropriate for a live listening feature.
